Identity Beyond Borders

Decentralized, biometric-secured digital identity for displaced civilians.

Create Your Identity

The Problem

Millions of people lose their identity during wars, disasters, and crises. Without identity, they struggle to access food, healthcare, education, and rights. SafeID ensures their identity stays safe — accessible anywhere, anytime.

How SafeID Works

1. Register

Sign up with basic details & secure your identity using your fingerprint.

2. Create Wallet

Your decentralized wallet stores encrypted documents & credentials.

3. Verify Anywhere

Access services with a fingerprint + QR code, anytime, anywhere.

The Impact

SafeID restores dignity and independence for displaced civilians.
A child can re-enroll in school, a patient can access healthcare, and families can receive aid — all with a fingerprint.
