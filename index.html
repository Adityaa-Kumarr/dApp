About Us.

SafeID is a blockchain-powered digital identity system designed to protect people during crises such as conflicts, natural disasters, or displacement. When traditional IDs are lost or inaccessible, SafeID ensures individuals can securely back up and recover their essential documents anytime, anywhere. All data is encrypted locally and stored on decentralized networks, making it private, tamper-proof, and portable across borders. With a simple wallet login or QR code, users can quickly verify their identity to access humanitarian aid, reunite with family, and restore their rights. SafeID is low-cost, scalable, and easy to use — built to give dignity, security, and hope to displaced civilians worldwide.
